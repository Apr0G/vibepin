Privacy & AI Policy

We don't store your images Photos you upload for virtual try-on or style analysis are processed in memory and discarded immediately after. We never save, log, or retain any images you submit.
AI-generated results are not perfect All results — product matches, style recommendations, and try-on previews — are generated by AI and may be inaccurate, incomplete, or not reflect real product availability. Use them as inspiration, not as a guarantee.
Pinterest profile analysis When you enter a Pinterest username, we fetch publicly visible pins from that profile to analyse your aesthetic. We only read public data and do not store your Pinterest credentials or profile information.
No account, no tracking vibepin does not require an account. We do not use cookies for tracking, sell your data to third parties, or build profiles on our users.
Third-party services We use third-party AI APIs to power our features. Your image data may be transiently processed by these services solely to generate results — refer to their respective privacy policies for details.
Not affiliated with Pinterest vibepin is an independent project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Pinterest, Inc.
